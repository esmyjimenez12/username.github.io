Esmy Jimenez is a journalist in Seattle, WA. Check out her latest work.

Projects

Periodistas Northwest was founded in 2019. Esmy is one of three original founders. The organization has over 20 members and is the newest chapter of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists. Periodistas Northwest's mission is to engage and support local Latinx and Hispanic journalists in newsrooms across Washington, Oregon, and Idaho.

Esmy invests in youth power. She is a mentor with Young Women Empowered, serving Black and brown youth in South Seattle. She also serves as a facilitator with KUOW's Radioactive youth radio program, teaching reporting skills to 15-18 year olds.
